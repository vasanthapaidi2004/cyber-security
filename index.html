Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Fake emails tricking you into revealing sensitive info.

Ransomware

Hackers lock your files and demand payment.

Malware

Malicious software designed to damage or steal data.

Social Engineering

Manipulating people to gain unauthorized access.

Best Practices

  • ✅ Use strong, unique passwords
  • ✅ Enable Two-Factor Authentication
  • ✅ Update software & devices regularly
  • ✅ Don’t click suspicious links
  • ✅ Backup your data securely
